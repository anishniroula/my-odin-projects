Thor's powers information

As the God of Thunder, Thor commands the skies, wielding the power of lightning to vanquish his foes and protect the realms.

Thor possesses superhuman strength, capable of shattering mountains, withstanding the force of a star, and battling the most formidable giants.

Only those deemed worthy can lift Mjolnir, the enchanted hammer that grants Thor unmatched strength, flight, and control over the elements.

With lightning-fast reflexes, Thor reacts with incredible speed and agility, dodging attacks and delivering swift strikes with his mighty hammer.

Whosoever holds this hammer, if they be worthy, shall possess the power of Thor.
- Thor, God of Thunder
